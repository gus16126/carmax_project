🔹 Core Topics Covered

Proper towing methods for electric vehicles

Flatbed towing vs. dolly towing

Avoiding drive motor and battery damage

Safety Precautions

Always place Tesla in “Transport Mode” (also known as “Tow Mode”) before loading.

Never tow with wheels on the ground (due to motor regen braking risk).

Disconnect high-voltage system when necessary.

Charging and Jump-Start Guidance

Accessing 12V battery and charge port if the car is unresponsive.

Powering on a Tesla with a depleted high-voltage battery for transport readiness.

Flatbed Procedure

Winching instructions using tow eye attachments.

Wheel strap positions and tire protection.

Prohibited Methods

No wheel-lift or chain towing.

Never drag a Tesla with wheels locked or with the transmission engaged.
